# Privacy Policy for YouTube Preference Engine

**Effective Date:** April 6, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Thank you for choosing to use the **YouTube Preference Engine** ("the Extension"). Your privacy is deeply important to us. This Privacy Policy details everything you need to know about how your data is collected, handled, stored, and shared when using the Extension. 

## 2. User Data Collection
The Extension works by building a profile of your YouTube viewing preferences to provide tailored Match % badges on videos.
- **What we access:** The Extension reads the titles, channel names, and topics of the YouTube videos you browse or interact with.
- **What we do NOT collect:** We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, physical address, or IP address. We do not track your browsing history outside of YouTube.

## 3. Data Handling
The data accessed by the Extension is processed **entirely locally on your device**.
- We use this viewing data solely for the purpose of running our scoring algorithm and calculating the relevance of recommended videos.
- If you have Chrome's Built-in AI enabled (such as Gemini Nano), the text data (video titles) is evaluated locally on your machine.
- No remote servers evaluate, process, or ever see your preferences or viewing habits.

## 4. Data Storage
- **Local Storage Only:** All data extracted (including your generated preference profile, channel watch counts, and session history) is stored exclusively in your browser's local secure storage (`chrome.storage.local`).
- **No Cloud Storage:** We do not host, store, or back up any of your data on our own remote servers or any third-party cloud.
- **Data Deletion:** Because the data is stored locally within the browser, you have full control over it. You can permanently delete all data collected by simply uninstalling the Extension or clearing your browser's extension data.

## 5. Data Sharing
- **No Data Sharing:** Since no data is ever transmitted from your device to our servers, we do not share, sell, rent, or trade your data to any third parties, advertisers, or analytics companies. 
- **Zero Third-Party Tracking:** We do not include any third-party trackers, analytics scripts (e.g., Google Analytics), or advertisement networks in this Extension.

## 6. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If any material changes are made, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy and provide an updated version in the Chrome Web Store.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page or submit an issue on our GitHub repository.
